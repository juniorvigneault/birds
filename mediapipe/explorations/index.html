Inner Birdsong is a research-creation project exploring the intersection of ML algorithms and anthropomorphism through the prism of humor theory. Utilizing MediaPipe's object detection tool on a continuous video feed, Inner Birdsong provides users with an intimate interactive window into the internal monologue of birds.
This project is part of my Master of Design thesis exploring the comedic potential of JavaScript frameworks.
